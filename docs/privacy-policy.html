Privacy Policy — Subscription Alert Last updated: 2025-12-24

This Privacy Policy explains how the Subscription Alert app (“App”) collects, uses, and shares information.

Quick summary
  • The App uses Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In) to sign you in.
  • Your subscription entries are stored in Cloud Firestore under your account so they sync across devices.
  • The App schedules local notifications on your device for reminders.
  • The App shows ads using Google AdMob (via Google Mobile Ads SDK).
  • If you connect your calendar, the App may access Google Calendar through Google APIs to help you add reminders/events.

2) Information we collect

2.1 Account & identity information

  • The App only uses this access when you explicitly use calendar-related features.
